Research Profile 科研概况

A research identity centred on evidence, reliability, and real-world validity. 以证据、可靠性与现实有效性为核心的研究取向。

I am an undergraduate researcher in computer science at Sichuan Agricultural University. Rather than treating benchmark improvement as the final goal, I am interested in whether a model is statistically reliable, clinically meaningful, robust under distribution shifts, and supported by reproducible evidence. My work spans medical image analysis, multimodal medical learning, and broader AI-for-Science problems. 我是四川农业大学计算机科学方向的本科生科研者。相比只追求基准指标提升， 我更关注模型是否具备统计可靠性、临床意义、分布偏移下的稳健性，以及是否 能够由可复现证据支持。我的研究覆盖医学图像分析、医学多模态学习，并延伸至 更广泛的科学智能问题。

Evidence before claims 证据先于结论 Conclusions should remain within what the experiments truly support. 研究结论应严格限定在实验真正支持的范围内。
Reliability before scale 可靠性先于规模 A smaller but auditable system is often more valuable than an opaque larger one. 一个规模较小但可审计的系统，往往比不可解释的大系统更有价值。
Mechanism before packaging 机制先于包装 Method design should be connected to the structure of the problem and domain. 方法设计应与问题本身及领域机制建立直接联系。

Research Directions 研究方向

A focused medical-AI core, with interdisciplinary extensions into AI for Science and embodied intelligence. 以医学人工智能为主线，并向科学智能与具身智能延伸。

Trustworthy Medical Image Analysis 可信医学图像分析

Reliability, uncertainty estimation, calibration, robustness, out-of-distribution evaluation, and clinically meaningful validation. 关注可靠性、不确定性估计、校准、稳健性、分布外评估与具有临床意义的验证。

Synthetic Medical Data & Quality Control 医学合成数据与质量控制

Validation-locked evaluation, data leakage auditing, safe augmentation, and anatomy- or physics-aware generation. 关注验证锁定评估、数据泄漏审计、安全数据增强，以及解剖或物理约束生成。

Medical Image Segmentation 医学图像分割

Small-sample learning, annotation-efficient methods, boundary modelling, and cross-domain generalization. 关注小样本学习、低标注学习、边界建模与跨域泛化。

Multimodal Medical Intelligence 医学多模态智能

Medical visual question answering, image–text learning, clinical report generation, and calibrated multimodal reasoning. 关注医学视觉问答、图文学习、临床报告生成与经过校准的多模态推理。

Research Perspective 科研理念

What I hope to contribute through research. 我希望通过科研持续回答的问题。
“

A useful model should not only perform well; it should also reveal when it may fail, why its evidence is credible, and whether its conclusions remain valid outside the benchmark. 一个真正有价值的模型，不仅要表现良好，还应说明它何时可能失败、 证据为何可信，以及其结论在离开基准数据后是否仍然成立。

“

I am interested in research that connects machine learning methods with medical, biological, or physical structure rather than treating domain data as interchangeable benchmarks. 我更关注能够把机器学习方法与医学、生物或物理结构真正联系起来的研究， 而不是把领域数据仅仅视为可以互换的基准测试。

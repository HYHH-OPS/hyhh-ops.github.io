About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher at Sichuan Agricultural University. My primary research interest is trustworthy medical artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on developing reliable, clinically meaningful, and auditable learning methods for medical imaging.

My current work covers medical image segmentation, synthetic-data quality control, uncertainty estimation and calibration, out-of-distribution generalization, and multimodal medical report generation. I am also interested in AI for Science and embodied intelligence.

Trustworthy Medical AI Medical Image Computing Uncertainty Calibration Synthetic Data Multimodal Learning AI for Science

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy Medical Image Analysis: reliability, uncertainty estimation, calibration, robustness, and out-of-distribution evaluation.
  • Synthetic Medical Data and Quality Control: clinically constrained generation, validation-locked evaluation, data leakage auditing, and safe data augmentation.
  • Medical Image Segmentation: small-sample learning, annotation-efficient learning, boundary modelling, and cross-domain generalization.
  • Multimodal Medical Intelligence: medical visual question answering, image-text learning, and medical report generation.
  • AI for Science and Embodied Intelligence: biological data modelling, scientific foundation models, multimodal perception, and reliable robotic interaction.

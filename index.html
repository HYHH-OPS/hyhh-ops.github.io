About 关于我

I am an undergraduate student at Sichuan Agricultural University. My current work examines how medical AI systems can be evaluated more rigorously under limited data, synthetic augmentation, uncertainty, and distribution shift. I value reproducible experiments, transparent reporting, and conclusions that remain within the evidence provided. 我是四川农业大学本科生。目前，我主要研究如何在有限数据、合成增强、 不确定性与分布偏移等条件下，更严谨地评估医学人工智能系统。我重视 可复现实验、透明报告，以及不超出证据边界的研究结论。

Research Interests 研究兴趣

Trustworthy Medical Image Analysis 可信医学图像分析

Reliability, uncertainty estimation, calibration, robustness, and out-of-distribution evaluation. 可靠性、不确定性估计、校准、稳健性与分布外评估。

Synthetic Medical Data and Quality Control 医学合成数据与质量控制

Safe augmentation, validation-locked evaluation, leakage auditing, and anatomy- or physics-aware generation. 安全数据增强、验证锁定评估、泄漏审计，以及解剖或物理约束生成。

Medical Image Segmentation 医学图像分割

Small-sample learning, annotation-efficient learning, boundary modelling, and cross-domain generalization. 小样本学习、低标注学习、边界建模与跨域泛化。

Multimodal Medical Learning 医学多模态学习

Medical visual question answering, image–text learning, and medical report generation. 医学视觉问答、图文学习与医学报告生成。
